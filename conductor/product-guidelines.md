# HabitFlow - Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Concise & Action-Oriented:** Communication should be brief, direct, and focused on helping the user complete their tasks efficiently.
- **Voice:** Professional and encouraging, without unnecessary filler.

## Visual Identity
- **Clean & Professional:** A balanced aesthetic that uses clear typography, professional color palettes, and structured layouts to instill confidence and focus.
- **Tone:** Reliable, modern, and high-quality.

## UX Principles
- **Speed & Efficiency:** Every interaction must be optimized for speed. Minimize the number of steps to perform common actions like tracking a habit.
- **Interactive Feedback:** Always provide immediate and meaningful feedback for user actions (e.g., toast notifications, haptic feedback, or visual transitions).
- **Accessibility First:** Ensure the application is usable by everyone. Follow WCAG standards for color contrast, screen reader compatibility, and keyboard navigation.

## Design System & Consistency
- **Component-Driven Development:** Reuse existing UI components to ensure a consistent look and feel throughout the application.
- **High-Quality Animations:** Use purposeful and smooth animations to enhance the user experience, provide context, and make the app feel "alive."
- **Standard Layouts:** Maintain consistent spacing, alignment, and grid systems across all screens and platforms.
