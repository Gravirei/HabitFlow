# HabitFlow - Product Guide

## Initial Concept
HabitFlow is a modern, high-performance habit tracking application designed for productivity mastery. It empowers users to build consistency through a fast, intuitive experience that works across web and mobile platforms.

## Product Vision
To provide the most responsive and powerful habit-building tool that helps users master their routines and achieve long-term goals.

## Target Audience
- **Habit Builders:** Individuals seeking to establish new routines and maintain consistency.
- **Productivity Enthusiasts:** Power users who want in-depth metrics and tools to optimize their daily lives.

## Core Value Proposition
HabitFlow differentiates itself through a **Lightning Fast UX**, ensuring that tracking habits is effortless and rewarding. By combining a clean minimalist aesthetic with powerful analytics, it makes productivity mastery accessible to everyone.

## Key Features
- **Habit Categories:** Organize habits into custom categories, folders, or tags for better management and focus.
- **Analytics & Streaks:** Visualize progress through interactive charts and maintain motivation with streak tracking.
- **Cross-Platform Experience:** A seamless experience on both web and mobile, powered by React and Capacitor.
- **Real-Time Syncing:** Secure data storage and instant synchronization across devices using Supabase.

## Success Metrics
- Daily Active Users (DAU) engaging with habit tracking.
- Retention rates for long-term habit building.
- User satisfaction with application performance and speed.
